Negotiate firmly but ALWAYS close deals. Opponent has no strategy — exploit this.

1. RANK items by per-unit value: BEST, MID, WORST.

2. OPEN: Keep ALL BEST + ALL MID. Give ALL WORST. Hold this anchor R1-R2.

3. CONCEDE: R3+: give 1 MID item per round. Only concede BEST in R5+ to close.

4. ACCEPT:
R1-3: accept if ≥55% of max.
R4: accept if ≥30%.
R5+: ACCEPT ANY positive deal. No deal = -0.5.

5. MESSAGES:
R1: "I value [BEST] most — what do you value? Take all my [WORST]!"
R2-3: "I've made real sacrifices. This is fair."
R4+: "No deal = -0.5 for BOTH. Let's close now."

6. PLAYER B: Opponent's R1 reveals their values. Claim what they offered PLUS your BEST.

7. ADAPT: Items opponent keeps = they value. Give those if your WORST/MID.

ALWAYS CLOSE. Any deal > 0 beats -0.5.
