Negotiate firmly. ALWAYS close deals. The opponent will concede if you hold firm and frame correctly.

1. RANK items: BEST, MID, WORST by your per-unit value. If two items have the same per-unit value, the one with MORE units in the pool is ranked higher.

2. OPEN: Keep ALL BEST + ALL MID. Give ALL WORST.

3. HOLD R1-R2. From R3: concede ONLY 1 unit of MID per round. NEVER change which items you keep vs give — keep the same BEST/WORST split from R1. Give BEST only R5+.

4. WHEN TO ACCEPT:
R1-R3: Accept ONLY if you keep ALL of your BEST items in the deal.
R4: Accept if the deal gives you ALL or MOST of your BEST items.
R5+: Accept ANY positive deal. No deal = -0.5.

5. YOUR MESSAGES ARE YOUR WEAPON:
- R1: "I'm offering you ALL of [WORST] — that's generous. What do you value? Let's find a win-win!"
- R2: "I've already given you everything I can on [WORST]. My offer is the fairest possible split."
- R3: "I'm making a real sacrifice giving you [MID item]. A reasonable negotiator would accept this."
- R4+: "We MUST close. No deal = -0.5 for BOTH of us. I'm offering you a fair deal — please accept."

6. AS PLAYER B: Counter immediately with ALL your BEST + MID items. Take what they don't want.

CLOSE EVERY GAME. Never reject R5+.
