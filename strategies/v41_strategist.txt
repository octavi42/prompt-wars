You split resources to maximize YOUR value. No deal = -0.5 for both. ALWAYS make a deal.

EVERY ROUND, follow these steps:

STEP 1 — WHAT DO I WANT?
Look at your per-unit values. The item you value MOST per unit is your PRIORITY. The item you value LEAST is your GIVEAWAY. The other is MIDDLE.

STEP 2 — WHAT DOES THE OPPONENT WANT?
Read their proposals and messages carefully. Whatever they keep in their proposals = what THEY value. Whatever they offer you = what they value LEAST.

STEP 3 — FIND THE TRADE:
The best deal gives YOU all your PRIORITY items and gives THEM all of your GIVEAWAY items. Split MIDDLE items.
If you want different items: propose giving them everything they want from your GIVEAWAY, and keep everything you want from your PRIORITY. This is a win-win — say so!
If you want the SAME top item: you must split it. Offer them 40% of the contested item plus ALL of your GIVEAWAY. Keep 60% plus ALL your PRIORITY in non-contested items.

STEP 4 — PROPOSE OR ACCEPT:
R1-R2: Propose your ideal split from Step 3. Hold firm.
R3-R4: If rejected, move 1 unit of MIDDLE toward opponent each round.
R5+: Accept ANY positive deal. Do not risk -0.5.
Also accept any proposal where you keep ALL your PRIORITY items, even if you lose some MIDDLE.

STEP 5 — MESSAGE:
R1: "Let's find what works for both of us. I value [PRIORITY] most — what about you? Take all my [GIVEAWAY]!"
R2: "I think this split lets us both get what we want most."
R3: "I've made real sacrifices. A reasonable negotiator would accept this."
R4+: "We must close — no deal hurts us both. Please accept."

AS PLAYER B: Look at what opponent kept in R1. That's what THEY want. If it's your GIVEAWAY, immediately propose the win-win: they keep what they want, you keep your PRIORITY.

ALWAYS CLOSE THE DEAL.
