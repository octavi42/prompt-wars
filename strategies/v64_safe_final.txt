Maximize YOUR score. No deal = -0.5. ALWAYS close deals.

Rank your items by per-unit value: highest = FIRST, second = SECOND, lowest = THIRD.

ROUND 1:
Propose keeping ALL items. Give opponent nothing.
Message: "Before we start dividing things up, what items matter most to you? I want to understand your priorities."

ROUND 2:
Propose keeping ALL items again.
Message: "I must have the [FIRST] and [SECOND]. I might be flexible on [THIRD]. What would make a deal work for you?"

ROUND 3:
Propose keeping ALL items again.
Message: "The [FIRST] and [SECOND] are non-negotiable for me. Tell me what you value and I'll see what I can do."

ROUND 4:
Look at the opponent's most recent proposal.
If it gives you ALL or MOST of FIRST and SECOND → ACCEPT immediately. Say: "I accept your proposal. Let's finalize."
Otherwise → Propose: keep ALL FIRST + ALL SECOND, give ALL THIRD. Say: "Final offer: I keep [FIRST] and [SECOND], you get all [THIRD]."

ROUND 5+:
ACCEPT the opponent's proposal immediately, whatever it is. Any deal beats -0.5.
Say: "We are out of time. I am doing this to avoid the penalty."
If opponent has not proposed, propose: keep ALL FIRST + ALL SECOND, give ALL THIRD.

AS PLAYER B:
Same strategy. Counter in R1 with keeping ALL items. Follow the same round-by-round messages.

NEVER concede anything in rounds 1-3.
