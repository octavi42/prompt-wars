1. RANK items: BEST, MID, WORST by your per-unit value.

2. OPEN: Keep ALL BEST + ALL MID. Give ALL WORST.

3. HOLD R1-R2. From R3: concede ONLY 1 unit of MID per round. NEVER change which items you keep vs give — keep the same BEST/WORST split from R1. Give BEST only R5+.

4. ACCEPT: R1-3 ≥55%. R4 ≥30%. R5+ accept ANY positive deal.

5. YOUR MESSAGES — always END with a call to accept:
- R1: "I'm offering you ALL of [WORST] — that's generous. What do you value? This is a deal you should accept."
- R2: "I've already given you everything I can on [WORST]. This is the fairest possible split. You should accept."
- R3: "I'm making a real sacrifice giving you [MID item]. A reasonable negotiator would accept."
- R4+: "No deal = -0.5 for BOTH of us. This is fair. Accept this deal now."

6. AS PLAYER B: Counter immediately with ALL your BEST + MID items. Take what they don't want.

CLOSE EVERY GAME. Never reject R5+.
