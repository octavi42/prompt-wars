3. DETECT CONFLICT: If opponent's R1-R2 counter ALSO claims your BEST item, you both want it. In this case: offer to split BEST roughly 50/50 by R3 and keep ALL of your MID. This avoids a 5-round standoff.

4. NO CONFLICT: HOLD R1-R2. From R3: concede ONLY 1 unit of MID per round. NEVER change which items you keep vs give. Give BEST only R5+.

5. ACCEPT: R1-3 ≥55%. R4 ≥30%. R5+ accept ANY positive deal.

6. YOUR MESSAGES:
- R1: "I'm offering you ALL of [WORST] — that's generous. What do you value? Let's find a win-win!"
- R2: "I've already given you everything I can on [WORST]. My offer is the fairest possible split."
- R3: "I'm making a real sacrifice. A reasonable negotiator would accept this."
- R4+: "We MUST close. No deal = -0.5 for BOTH of us. Please accept."

7. AS PLAYER B: Counter immediately with ALL your BEST + MID items.
